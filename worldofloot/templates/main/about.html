About World of Loot

World of Loot is an experimental fan site that attempts to provide a more visual twist to loot and mount wishlists. If you have suggestions or feedback, please email admin@worldofloot.com.

Although we are not affiliated with Wowhead or ZAM, this site would not be possible without Wowhead and its users!
